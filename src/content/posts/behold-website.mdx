I've been meaning to actually put something attached to my domain on the internet for *checks notes* 15 years now. It's funny, I think I visited [boek.us](https://boek.us) for the first time in years to find a time when I was playing around with [Deno Deploy](https://deno.com/deploy).

## Why Now?
Well... I have something next week that is forcing me to re-familiarize myself with a <del>JavaScript</del> TypeScript environment. The last time I seriously did web development [atom](https://atom-editor.cc) and [Sublime Text](https://www.sublimetext.com) were the defacto editors. There weren't things like LSPs and TypeScript had just been released.

It's kind of wild. things are _easier_ than ever, but on the flipside also the layers of abstraction are so complex that I spent 45 minutes trying to find out why I couldn't get the <LspError>red squigglys</LspError> to go away.

Now I wouldn't consider myself a prodigy, but I've been doing this for a while and getting stuck like that killed my ego 😂

But the fact that I could go from nothing to having this deployed in a few hours is just amazing me. I'm having a blast. I'm not sure that I'm going to continue writing but even if I don't this is better than [what was there before](https://web.archive.org/web/20240803084706/https://boek.us/).
